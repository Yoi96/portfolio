  • Genshin Impact Item Model
  • Date: 2022
  • SOFTEWARES: Blender
  • Game Item: Genshin Impact's Acquaint Fate or Intertwined Fate
Creating a detailed 3D model of the Acquaint Fate or Intertwined Fate items from the popular game Genshin Impact using Blender was both a challenging and rewarding experience.

To start, I immersed myself in the world of Genshin Impact, studying the design and significance of these items. Acquaint Fate and Intertwined Fate are pivotal for players as they are used to make wishes, hoping to obtain new characters or weapons. Acquaint Fate is used for standard wishes, while Intertwined Fate is used for event wishes. Both have a mystical and ethereal design, featuring intricate patterns, glowing elements, and a combination of celestial motifs.

The first step in Blender was to create the base shape of the items. Both Fates have a circular core, so I began by modeling a sphere and then flattened it slightly to resemble the reference images. For Acquaint Fate, I added the distinctive star-shaped patterns by creating star meshes and carefully placing them around the core. For Intertwined Fate, I focused on the ribbon-like structures that wrap around the core, which required careful manipulation of bezier curves to get the flowing, intertwined effect.

Detailing was crucial to capture the essence of these mystical items. Using Blender's sculpting tools, I carved intricate patterns onto the surface of the models. This step was meticulous, ensuring each line and curve matched the ornate designs from the game. Texturing was the next significant phase. I used a combination of Blender's built-in materials and custom textures created in an external software like Photoshop. For Acquaint Fate, the textures needed to reflect a cool, celestial blue with subtle glows, while Intertwined Fate required a more vibrant, magical hue with dynamic glowing effects.

Creating these models in Blender was a journey of creativity and technical skill. It required a deep understanding of the game's art style and meticulous attention to detail to replicate the intricate designs. The project enhanced my 3D modeling skills and gave me a greater appreciation for the artistry involved in game design. Each step, from conceptualization to the final render, was a testament to the power of digital art in bringing virtual items to life.
