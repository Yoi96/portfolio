  • Logo DESIGN
  • Date: 2016
  • Elements: @yoi
  • SOFTEWARES: Adobe Photoshop & Paint Tool SAI

The @yoi logo is designed to serve as a versatile and visually appealing watermark for backgrounds. This logo creatively combines the distinct elements of a demon and a cat, resulting in a unique and memorable design that enhances various media and materials without overpowering the primary content.

Design Elements

@ Symbol Integration:

The core of the logo is the @ symbol, which is central to its identity. This symbol is carefully crafted to incorporate the brand's unique elements.

Rectangular "O" with Cat Features:

The "O" within the @ symbol is transformed into a rectangular shape. Inside this rectangle, the cute features of a cat, including expressive eyes, a small nose, and a mouth, are prominently displayed. These features add a touch of charm and playfulness, making the logo endearing and engaging.

Demon's Trident as "Y":

The letter "Y" in @yoi is designed to resemble a demon's trident. This trident adds a dynamic and mystical element to the logo. Its prongs extend outward, integrating seamlessly with the @ symbol and providing a sense of power and intrigue.

Demon's Tail as "i":

The letter "i" is cleverly incorporated as a demon's tail. The tail's flowing, sinuous line wraps around the @ symbol, reinforcing the demon imagery and adding another layer of visual interest.

Watermark Application

As a watermark, the @yoi logo is designed to be subtle yet distinctive, enhancing the background without distracting from the main content. Here’s how it can be effectively used:

Opacity and Size: The logo can be rendered in a semi-transparent form, ensuring it blends smoothly with various background textures and colors. Adjusting the size appropriately allows it to be visible yet unobtrusive.

Repetition and Placement: The logo can be repeated in a pattern across the background or placed strategically in corners or along the edges to maintain a consistent brand presence without overwhelming the viewer.

Versatility: The design’s balance of playful cat features and mysterious demon elements ensures it works well across different media, from digital screens to printed materials.

Conclusion

The @yoi logo, with its combination of a rectangular "O" featuring a cat's face, a trident-like "Y," and a demon's tail "i," is an ideal watermark for backgrounds. Its thoughtful design ensures it enhances the visual appeal of any surface while subtly reinforcing the brand identity. This creative and versatile logo is perfect for making a lasting impression as a watermark, adding both charm and intrigue to the visual experience.
