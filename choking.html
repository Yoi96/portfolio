Choking First Aid Posters

Software: Adobe Photoshop & Adobe Indesign

Class: ART29510_Graphic Design Concepts

Date: 2023 (Spring)

This project has 3 parts.
★ One is picture and text.
★ Secend is text only.
★ The last one is picture only.
 
Picture and text choking first aid poster.
★ Color of clothes of two cartoon characters.
★ Cartoon posters often feature bright colors and eye-catching graphics, making them easily recognizable. This helps attract people's attention, especially in emergency situations.
★Cartoon posters typically use simple images and language that are understandable by people of all age groups. This means that both children and the elderly can comprehend and use these posters during emergencies.
 
Text only choking first aid poster.
★ Using red color to highlight important sentences, so people can read it clearly.
★ Versatility: These posters can be easily adapted for different situations, from medical emergencies to natural disasters, as the information can be changed and updated easily.
★ Cost-Effective: Creating text-based posters is often more cost-effective than designing and printing posters with graphics or illustrations. They can be produced quickly and at a lower cost.
★ Consistency: Text-based information is less likely to be misinterpreted compared to visuals, ensuring that the provided information remains consistent and accurate.
 
Picture only choking first aid poster.
★ I arranged the images in the shape of the number 5 to illustrate that there are five steps in the choking first aid procedure.
★ The graphics are divided into two sections: the first part displays the number 5 arranged from right to left, illustrating how bystanders can assist in first aid when someone is choking. The bottom part of the number 5 outlines the steps for providing immediate aid to a choking individual. Both sections conclude with the step of dialing 911.
