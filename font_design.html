Designed in my Typography I class. I like use sans-serif type, so my font design also uses sans-serif style. You also can download it with link. I designed 3 style poster designs.
 

★ First poster is using A-Z words in the background, and using the blue, white, yellow, and black colors.

★ Xiaoyi sans is in yellow, which looks clear and obvious against the blue background and white font.

★ Quotes: "The bad news is time flies. The good news is you're the pilot." by Michael Altshuler means that time passes quickly, but you have the power to control how you spend your time. It is up to you to decide how you want to use your time and what you want to achieve in life. Therefore, I uses blue color background makes people remind the sky and pilot.

★ In addition, I put "XY" alone to mean my name Xiaoyi, and the design of X is reminiscent of a propeller or fan.

 

★ This design is a text dialog box, which means that we can use this font as a mobile font.

★ The colors I used are yellow, red and blue, which are reminiscent of the game, and the dot hollows of the font are reminiscent of the Pac-Man game.

★ Simple design.

 

★ This design is using Aa-Zz words to make into petals, and font name is the flower core.

★ I only used white, black, and red colors because when I designed it to resemble a flower, it reminded me of a manga titled The Flowers of Evil.
